About Our Pizzas

Our pizzas are made with ingredients imported directly from Italy. We use San Marzano tomatoes, fresh buffalo mozzarella, and our dough is fermented for 48 hours for optimal flavor and texture.

All pizzas are cooked in our traditional wood-fired oven at 900°F for just 90 seconds, giving them the perfect char and crispiness.

About Our Desserts

Our desserts are crafted using traditional Italian recipes passed down through generations. We use only the finest ingredients, including imported Italian mascarpone, cocoa, and espresso.

Our gelato is churned slowly to create a denser, creamier texture with more intense flavors than regular ice cream.
